# Brand Context (example — replace with your own)

`/carousel` reads this for the voice and aesthetic of your slide copy. Keep it short and concrete.

## Brand name + handle
Your name / brand. Your Instagram handle (goes on the closer slide).

## Aesthetic
The look your covers and pillar images should hit. Color palette, mood, references. Example: "Dark, neon-purple accents, cinematic, high-contrast. Gaming / shadow aesthetic."

## Voice
How the slide copy reads. Example: "Punchy, builder-energy, contrast-driven. One ALL-CAPS word per body line for emphasis."

## Audience
Who you're talking to. Their pain, their goal.

## Recurring CTA keywords + deliverables
The lead magnets you point people to (matches the Gift slide). Example: keyword `AGENT` → "the full Claude Code toolkit".

## Highlight color
The accent color used for the one highlighted word in each pillar headline. Sample the exact hex from your Canva template (the reference deck uses an orange ~`#F26B3A`).
